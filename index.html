Welcome to a website all about Howard's van, it's conversion and it's travels

Hello and welcome, this website is a continuous document recording the history of the van including it's repair and conversion work, as well any trips or voyages it has embarked on. Mostly this is as a practice for the HTML coding skills I learnt over lockdown so as the entire thing wasn't a complete waste of time.

So without firther or do, I introduce the van in it's current state

Having clocked up the hours working on the van over the second lockdown in November, the front kitchen setup has come along well, however I am finding out how every little thing is just taking a long time. For example where the edges are blended from living space I have created to the odd curves and corners of the van or the doors. I think the worst is probably the trasition from the cockpit in the front to the kitchen area, as it's not easy to just hide with the speaker mesh fabric I had done elsewhere as there's nowhere to pin it behind. It's also proving a pain because how the blind will fix across with part of the sliding door rail in the way at the top is also a dilemma.
