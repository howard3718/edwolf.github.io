Finances

Below is a pretty detailed summary of each of the costs associated with the van, split into three main categories:

  1. Repair - costs associated with the intial repair to the wing of the van
  2. Conversion - all the costs associated with converting the van to a camper
  3. Running - asociated with simply owning the van (insurance, maintenance etc.)

This is on the off chance you are thinking of a conversion yourself and want a rough guide. It's also why I've kept the costs so granular so you can see exactly where they've come from and those which you might not incur/anything I scavanged for free but you would have to purchase.
