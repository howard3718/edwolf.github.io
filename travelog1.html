Italy/Germany 2020

This page details a trip taken by Ben, Arjun and myself to Italy & Germany in August 2020 between lockdowns 1&2. I also produced a trip video which is featured in the page.

Rush to Leave (Fri)

As always I was deadline pushing and blaming it on perfection before we left. I only started painting and laquering 2 days before we left and putting the doors and catches back on the day before. However by the Friday morning I had managed to get both walls painted and installed, a first attempt at the floor and a terrible bed in place. I also stole a table frame from the yard and chucked in the half finished mattress and we set off. However we ended up returning twice, once half an hour in to get the bedding, and another after 200m as the crockery and cutlery I had ordered was delivered. Eventually we just about made our shuttle and then Ben and I rushed across France & Belgium to Cologne.
